General Purpose Unsupervised Learning

The core of our breakthrough technology is a general-purpose unsupervised learning (GPUL) engine.

  • It outputs a hierarchical abstraction of the data.

  • It works on data of different modalities.

  • It works on data of different dimensions.

  • It can learn both category hierarchy and structure hierarchy.

  • It is based on one core algorithm.

In summary, our unsupervised learning engine can learn autonomously from data of different modalities and dimensions, including tabular data, graph data, image, language, etc. It serves as the core of the our analytics service platform.

Applications

There are many practical applications of our technology. We are just touching the surface. The areas we are focusing on include data mining and pattern discovery, biomedical image analysis, scene understanding, self-driving technologies, and etc. We will update our progress on our blog series.
